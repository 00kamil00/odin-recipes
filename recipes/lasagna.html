Lasagna Recipe

Desctription

A classic Italian baked pasta dish made of alternating layers of rich,
slow-simmered Bolognese (meat and tomato) sauce and creamy Béchamel (white) sauce,
topped with a thick, bubbly layer of melted golden cheese.

Ingredients

  • 500 g minced meat (beef or beef and pork mix)
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 can of chopped tomatoes
  • 3 teaspoons of tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon of Italian herbs
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 2 tablespoons of all-purpose flour
  • 500 ml milk
  • 9-12 dry lasagna pasta sheets
  • 200 g grated cheese (mozzarella or gouda)
  • A pinch of salt
  • A pinch of black pepper
  • A pinch of ground nutmeg

Steps

  1. Sautéing the base: Fry the chopped onion and garlic in a pan, add the minced meat, and cook until browned.
  2. Simmering the sauce: Add the canned tomatoes, tomato paste, and herbs; simmer on low heat for 15 minutes until thick.
  3. Making the roux: Melt the butter in a saucepan, add the flour, and cook for one minute, stirring constantly.
  4. Cooking the béchamel: Slowly pour in the cold milk while whisking vigorously; cook on low heat until it thickens like pudding, then season with salt, pepper, and nutmeg.
  5. Preheating: Preheat your oven to 180°C (fan oven).
  6. Bottom layer: Spread a thin layer of béchamel sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  7. Layering: Lay down pasta sheets, then a layer of meat sauce, béchamel, and a little bit of cheese.
  8. Repeating: Repeat the sequence (pasta-meat-béchamel-cheese) 3-4 times.
  9. Top layer: Cover the final layer of pasta with only the remaining béchamel and cover generously with the rest of the cheese.
  10. Baking: Bake for 30-35 minutes until the cheese is golden and the pasta is soft.
  11. Resting: Remove from the oven and let it rest for 10 minutes before cutting so the lasagna doesn't fall apart.
